Group project: Toto To

team pelican Celebrating Places

    Toto Too is a LGBTQ+ charity in the North East that prides on highlighting safe and inclusive spaces in and around Newcastle, we are committed to supporting queer youth in the North East. The organization is formed around LGBTQ+ communities, our overall aim is to provide support for young people, by celebrating queer individuals we create community and will provide resources and support directly to youth, including identifying safe/welcoming spaces, events, people, and advice.

    We designed an informative map to tell you about exciting upcoming events in our favourite safe places.
